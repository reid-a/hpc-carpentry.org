Our Maintainers

HPC Carpentry Maintainers work with the community to make sure that lessons stay up-to-date, accurate, functional and cohesive. Maintainers can expect to acquire this skill set. To become a maintainer Contact us.

This page lists the Maintainers who have already consented to be listed on our website. If you are a Maintainer whose details do not appear here, but you would like to be listed, please Contact us.

About HPC Carpentry

HPC Carpentry is a set of teaching materials designed to help new users take advantage of high-performance computing systems. No prior computational experience is required - these lessons are ideal for either an in-person workshop or independent study. More ›
